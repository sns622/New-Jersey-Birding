NEW JERSEY BIRDING

Have you ever looked up at the sky and been curious about the animals soaring overhead or perching in a tree? If so, this site is your guide to discovering the world of birding in New Jersey. Whether you're completely new or simply looking to broaden your knowledge, this site will provide you with all the information you need to begin your birdwatching journey. Get started by visiting our "Intro to Birding" page for a clear overview on the topic and tips on becoming a birder. You can also explore our bird identification section, where we highlight some of the most common birds you might see around the state and provide you with basic information about them. Lastly, if you're looking to join a community of likeminded people, or simply want to reach out with inquiries, head over to our contact page and fill out the form. And don't forget to follow the link to join our Discord server!
